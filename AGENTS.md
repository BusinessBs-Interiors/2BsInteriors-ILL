# 2B's Interior Construction Development Guidelines

## Official Brand Assets

### Logo Images
Whenever you need to display, reference, or update the company logo for 2B'S Interior Construction, you **MUST** point to the official web assets repository:
- **Repository URL:** `https://github.com/BusinessBs-Interiors/Web-Assets`
- **Primary Logo Raw Link:** `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/BusinessBs-Interiors/Web-Assets/main/Logo.jpeg`

### Usage Rules
- Do not download, copy, or use outdated local placeholders or older alternative repositories (such as the legacy `2Bsadmin-sudo/2Bs-Interior-Git` logo assets).
- Maintain high-quality presentation, ensuring proper rendering context (`referrerPolicy="no-referrer"`) and clean margins/padding.
